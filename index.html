Recent Projects
The Shapes of Smell, 2025
A scent can hold an entire moment: coffee in the morning, a familiar flower, the warmth of freshly ironed fabric.
This window installation at Smell Stories transforms fragrances into sculptural forms. Through the Smell Generator, visitors discover a scent’s drawn shape, spray the matching perfume, and take home a small scented memory.
J'ai perdu mes clés, 2025
Have you ever lost your keys, eaten pasta with tomato sauce until your mouth turned orange, smoked a cigarette, cracked an egg, danced in cowboy boots?
This exhibition and window display at Chien Andalou, a coffee and art space, unfolds sculptural scenes in papier-mâché. Everyday gestures and familiar objects become intimate installations, inviting visitors to reconnect with their own memories.
Petit Déjeuner, 2025
An installation and performance exploring the poetry of everyday life. Papier-mâché objects and staged gestures transform ordinary moments into intimate, playful scenes.
Piknikdouille, 2025
A drag performance takes the form of a picnic in the park. In collaboration with drag artist Kidzmoon, the project unfolds a playful and strange story, blending performance, storytelling, and outdoor spectacle.

Lives and works in Brussels.

Ulrike creates sculptures and objects in papier-mâché, often used in scenography, window displays, and decor for films or photoshoots. Her work combines playful forms, vibrant colors, and everyday details, exploring how objects can be touched, worn, or used in different contexts.
